Welcome!

At SpeechMania, we believe everyone deserves to be able to communicate with others through language. Speaking, understanding, reading, writing, gesturing and using numbers affects every aspect of success in school, in work, and in relationships. Today, one response could be the needed one to make greater use of digital technologies trying to improve outcomes and contain costs.

Our mission is to introduce an interactive new technology-based system for Aphasia rehabilitation overcoming the deficiencies in traditional rehabilitation. We're excited to provide a language virtual reality (VR)-based environment that provides the flexibility and convenience of getting therapy sessions at home. Our system is validated to result in a faster word retreival and improve language ability and life quality.
